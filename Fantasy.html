What is Fantasy Cricket?

Fantasy cricket is an online game wherein you create your own team of players from the pool of players available in an upcoming real-life match. The process is very simple, you have to score as many points as possible and beat your opponents to win.

You have to pick the best team from the players of the two teams playing a match on the given date. Then you get to select a captain and vice-captain from the group of players you have chosen before you lock the team. The points are distributed depending on the player's performance in the real life match. There are different rules and set of points awarded for runs, wickets, catches, among other parameters.

Fantasy cricket is a game involving a lot of careful analysis and good knowledge of the sport. The selection of your ideal Fanatsy-XI depends on multiple factors including the pitch, weather conditions, ground size, current form of players, the toss etc.

How to Create Team

You can only pick a maximum of 7 players from one team. Your fantacy cricket team needs to constitute of the following.

Wicket-Keepers(Minimum-1, Maximum-4)
Batsmen(Minimum-3, Maximum-6)
All-rounders( Minimum-1, Maximum-4)
Bowlers(Minimum-3, Maximum-6)

Player Combinations

on selecting the 11 players, you will need to assign a caption and vice- caption

The caption will give you 2X points scored by them in the actual match.

The vuce caption will give you 1.5X points scored by them in the actual match.

Once you have chosen your combination, save your team, and you are good to go. Starting points are assigned to any player based on the announcement of his/her inclusion in the team. You should pick players that you feel will earn maximum points on the given day.
